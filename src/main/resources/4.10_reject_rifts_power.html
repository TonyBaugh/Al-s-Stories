The Rift is Denied—Destruction or Balance

The Rift hums, deep and resonant, a force that has woven itself into every choice, every war, every life touched by its influence. It does not ask for acceptance, nor does it demand submission—it simply is.

But you refuse.

The weight of its presence bears down upon you, pressing into your mind, into your bones. The shifting air crackles with violet light, warping the ground at your feet, urging you forward. But you do not move. You do not reach out.

You deny it.

Something shifts. The Rift flickers—just for an instant. Its pulsing energy falters, its colors bleeding into one another, uncertain, unstable. The world around you quivers in response, as though the very fabric of reality is listening to your defiance.

It has never been rejected before.

The tendrils of energy that once reached for you hesitate, curling back as if unsure. The visions that have clawed at the edges of your thoughts—glimpses of power, of eternity—fracture, growing distant. The Rift is testing you, waiting for you to yield.

But you will not.

A low, resonant tremor rolls through the ground, deep and thunderous, as if the Rift itself is protesting your choice. The sky above you fractures, streaks of gold and black slashing through the Rift’s ever-shifting hues. The ground beneath you cracks, revealing slivers of light—something deeper, something beneath even this.

A voice—wordless, ancient—presses against the edges of your thoughts.

You do not understand what you are doing.

But that is where it is wrong.

You understand more than anyone ever has.

For the first time, the Rift is not something unknowable, something beyond reach. It is just another force. Another power. Another thing that can be ended.

And so, you force it back.

A blinding surge of energy erupts around you as the Rift recoils, a howl of pure force reverberating through existence itself. The structures around you—the broken ruins of a world shaped by the Rift’s touch—shudder and crack, some collapsing into dust, others twisting into new, unfamiliar forms. The Rift fights against your will, struggling to maintain its hold.

You can feel its desperation now.

It does not want to fade.

But it will.

Now, standing at the precipice of this unraveling world, you face the last decision.
