Destruction: Unleash the Final Blow

The Rift shifts, the colors within its depths twisting in restless spirals—violet fractures against black, gold bleeding through like dying embers.

It knows what you are about to do.

It does not resist.

Your breath is steady as you lift your weapon, your hands trembling not from fear, but from the sheer weight of this moment. The Rift has endured beyond the span of history, beyond the lifetimes of those who dared to name it. It has rewritten the fates of countless worlds, shaped and unmade civilizations, bent time itself to its unknowable will.

And yet, here you stand.

The last being who will ever challenge it.

The sky fractures above, cracks of molten red splitting through a voidless abyss. The ground beneath you trembles, veins of golden light seeping through its fractures like the final heartbeat of a dying world.

Ilvane’s voice cuts through the rising storm. “If you do this… you might not survive.”

You already know.

A deep, resonant pulse ripples through the air—slow, measured. The Rift’s hum is no longer chaotic, no longer unstable. It is calm. As if waiting for something inevitable. As if it always knew this moment would come.

Perhaps it did.

You tighten your grip. No hesitation. No turning back.

You strike.

Blinding light erupts, not as an explosion, but as a collapse—a force so absolute that space itself folds inward, devouring the Rift from within. The warping light twists in upon itself, its core flickering like a dying star. The Rift screams, a soundless cry that tears through existence, shaking the very fabric of reality.

The echoes of past voices—the warriors, the scholars, the Riftborn, the ones who fought and fell before—shatter like glass, their whispers scattered to nothingness. The Rift does not lash out. It does not retaliate.

It only ends.

And so do you.

The power surges through your veins, an unbearable force, a light too vast for mortal flesh to contain. Your body fractures, the golden energy seeping into your skin, your bones dissolving into something far beyond pain. You are not simply breaking.

You are becoming part of the Rift’s final breath.

The last thing you hear is Ilvane’s voice, distant, desperate, grieving.

Then—

Silence.

The Rift is gone.

The war is over.

The world, once held hostage by its presence, breathes again. The sky smooths into a deep, endless twilight, the air no longer trembling beneath an unseen force. The weight that loomed over existence for countless ages lifts, leaving behind only quiet, only stillness.

But you are not there to see it.

You are nothing.

You are everything.

You are gone.

Summary – Destruction: Unleash the Final Blow

The Rift has been destroyed—not by war, not by control, but by absolute annihilation. Its presence, its influence, its very existence has been erased. But the cost was final.

You do not walk away from this battle. You do not claim victory. You do not witness the world freed from the Rift’s grasp.

Because you are gone.

What This Means for the Player
  • This is the ultimate sacrifice. You chose to erase the Rift at the cost of your own existence.
  • The world is free, but it will never know the price you paid.
  • Unlike other endings, there is no future for you. No legend. No return.
  • The Rift is gone, and with it, so are you.

You ended the Rift. But in the end, it took you with it.
