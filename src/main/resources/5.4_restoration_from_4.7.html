Restoration: Seal the Rift Forever

The Rift does not fight you. It does not resist. It does not beg.

It only continues to give.

The weight of it crushes your mind, an avalanche of knowledge, a tide that will not cease. The history of the world—of every world—pours through you in an unrelenting stream. Truths that no civilization was meant to hold. Patterns that stretch beyond the concept of time itself. The understanding of everything.

And yet—

You cannot hold it all.

Your thoughts splinter, unraveling into fragments of a self that is no longer wholly human. The Rift will take you, will make you more, if you allow it. But you feel the edges of yourself slipping, fading. If you do not stop this now, you will not survive it.

Somewhere in the distance, a voice is calling your name. Distant, familiar, sharp with desperation. It cuts through the flood of knowledge like a blade, an anchor pulling you back toward something small, something fragile, something mortal.

Your own humanity.

You focus on it, reaching for yourself amid the storm of knowing, forcing the Rift’s presence back—not rejecting it, but containing it, shaping it, bending it into something that can be controlled.

And for the first time—the Rift hesitates.

A ripple of resistance moves through its depths, the vast and infinite threads of possibility tightening, constricting. It has never been denied before. It does not understand refusal.

It does not understand.

But you do.

Your hands burn with power, your mind screaming as you force the Rift closed, not through destruction, but through finality. You weave the energy inward, severing its reach, folding its limitless potential into something finite—something that can never be used, never be touched again.

It resists. It fights. But it cannot stop you.

The sky fractures, the swirling constellations within the Rift flickering out like dying embers. The hum that has reverberated through the fabric of existence for as long as history remembers begins to dim, to fade. The Rift is collapsing, sealing itself away, its limitless energy locked beyond the veil of reality.

And then—

Silence.

You stagger, your body barely holding together, your mind frayed at the edges, but you are here. The Rift is no more. The wound in reality has been closed—not destroyed, not erased, but contained.

The world is still. The weight of the Rift’s pull is gone.

For the first time, in an eternity that stretches beyond mortal reckoning, the Rift is silent.

And you have won.

Summary – Sealing the Rift

The Rift is no longer a threat. It is no longer a force waiting to consume or control. You have sealed it away forever, ensuring that no one will ever wield its power or risk its destruction again.

But the cost is undeniable—you have seen beyond the limits of human comprehension, and some part of you will never return.

The world will move forward, free from the Rift’s influence.

But will it be enough?

What This Means for the Player
  • The Rift has been contained, not destroyed, ensuring its power will never be unleashed again.
  • Unlike the destruction endings, the world remains intact—but you have touched something beyond mortal understanding.
  • You succeeded, but at a cost. Some part of you, something beyond words, has changed.
  • The world continues, unaware of what you have done, unable to grasp the magnitude of what was at stake.

The Rift remains—hidden, silent, waiting.

And only you will remember why.
