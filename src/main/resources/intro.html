=== Introduction ===

A deep, unshakable cold clings to your skin as you awaken on the ground, the texture beneath you neither grass nor stone, but something unplaceable - smooth and shifting, like frozen silk. Your breath fogs in the dim air.

Above you, the sky is wrong. A swirling Rift, an enormous tear in reality itself, pulses with hues you have no name for - blues that burn, violets that shimmer, gold that flickers like dying embers.

"It's you. Finally, you've returned. But which side are you on this time?"

The others murmur, conflicting whispers laced with awe and dread. A woman grips the hilt of a blade at her side, knuckles white. A faint violet glow dances across her fingers, a power barely restrained. She exhales sharply.

"You don't remember, do you?"

"What you are? What you've done?"

The air crackles as the Rift pulses again. A wave of nausea washes over you, disorienting and violent, as if reality itself is shifting beneath you.

For a moment - just a moment - you see something else. Flashes of crimson fire, a city crumbling, hands reaching toward you, pleading.

And then - nothing.

The world holds its breath, waiting.

You feel it - the weight of a past you cannot recall pressing against you, demanding a choice.
