If No One Understands It, Will You?

The Rift looms before you—

a wound in the fabric of reality,

pulsing with unnatural color—

hues beyond mortal perception,

twisting between shades of violet fire and ink-black nothingness.

The air vibrates with a frequency

too low to hear,

but you feel it—

deep in your bones,

as if something beneath the surface of reality itself

is stirring.

You stand on the precipice,

your presence acknowledged by the impossible force ahead.

The land surrounding the Rift is a graveyard of forgotten attempts—

shattered weapons half-embedded in molten stone,

structures warped into spirals of fused glass and bone,

footprints that end abruptly

where the ground dissolves into pure absence.

The scholars warned against this.

The warriors whispered of those who stepped too close

and never returned.

But none of them truly know,

because none have dared to do

what you are about to do.

The Rift pulses again.

It is aware of you.

The first step forward is excruciating—

the air thickens,

pressing against your body like unseen hands,

dragging at your limbs

as if time itself resists your intrusion.

You push forward.

The second step steals the breath from your lungs.

The sky fractures,

the stars above rearranging into constellations

you have never seen before.

The Rift shifts,

its swirling abyss no longer chaotic,

but structured—

angled,

as if it is shaping itself into something deliberate.

By the third step,

your vision splits.

You see yourself.

Not just one reflection,

but many—

a thousand versions of you,

flickering like shattered glass

caught in an unseen wind.

Some clad in armor,

some in rags,

some hold weapons slick with blood,

others extend their hands toward the Rift,

mirroring your hesitation.

Every possibility.

Every choice you could have made,

have made,

will make.

They all speak at once.

"You were always meant to be here."

Their voices crash over you

like a tidal wave,

an echo of something vast,

something that stretches beyond time itself.

For the first time, you understand—

this is not just an anomaly.

It is an intelligence.

An entity.

A force that has been reaching across existence,

shaping destinies in ways no one could have imagined.

And now—

it reaches for you.

The Rift surges,

tendrils of raw energy spiraling outward,

weaving through the air

like living threads of pure creation.

They do not attack.

They do not consume.

They offer.

A choice.
