Will You Become Something New?

The Rift calls.

Not with words, not with force—

but with inevitability.

Every step you have taken has led here.

Every battle, every question,

every act of defiance or curiosity

has carved a path to this moment.

The Rift looms before you, shifting, alive.

It is no longer an anomaly,

no longer a mistake.

It is something that was always meant to be.

And perhaps…

So were you.

The air around the Rift is thick with color—

violet folding into deep indigos,

radiant gold streaks pulsing like veins beneath its shifting surface.

The closer you step, the more your body hums with its energy.

It reaches for you,

threads of luminous light stretching out,

winding around your fingers,

your arms,

your mind.

It is not pulling you in.

It is welcoming you.

A voice drifts through the air, weightless yet undeniable.

"You see it now, don’t you?"

You turn.

But the speaker is not there.

Or maybe they are.

Maybe they always were.

The Rift pulses again, and in its glow, shapes shift.

Shadows lengthen,

flickering between human forms and something else—

beings of energy,

beings of motion and thought

without flesh to contain them.

One figure steps forward.

Their body flickers at the edges,

their face familiar yet unknowable.

They are not Riftborn.

They are not human.

They are between.

"You were never meant to be separate from it,"

they murmur.

"None of us were."

The Rift hums.

You step forward, and the color bleeds into your skin—

gold streaking through your veins,

violet threading through your fingertips,

something shifting within your bones.

The Rift does not force.

It does not demand.

It offers.
