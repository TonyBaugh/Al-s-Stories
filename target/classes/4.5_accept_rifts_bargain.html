You Have Uncovered Something Never Meant to Be Known

The Rift is no longer just a force—

It is watching.

Every step, every battle, every unraveling truth—

It has all led to this.

And now, the Rift has decided to speak.

A low hum fills the air—

deeper than sound, deeper than thought,

deeper than you.

The glow of the Rift pulses in slow, deliberate waves.

Measured.

Aware.

Waiting.

A crack splits the ground beneath your feet.

Gold-veined, pulsing like an open wound.

Something shifts.

Not a figure, not a voice—

but a presence.

It coils around you, formless yet heavy,

pressing against your skin.

A whisper threads through your mind.

"We are past the point of choice. Now, you must decide."

And suddenly—

you are not here.

You stand in a place that should not exist.

Not past. Not present. Not future.

A space between moments, between realities.

The sky stretches in impossible directions,

ink-black, burning with violet constellations

that pulse in time with your heartbeat.

Before you, towering and shifting,

is the Rift itself.

Not as a wound.

Not as an accident.

As something designed.

"You were never meant to reach this point."

The voice is layered,

fractured,

a thousand voices woven into one.

The Rift moves,

twisting, stretching, until it forms

something almost humanoid.

A reflection of what you could become.

"You have gone too far."

It does not feel like a warning.

It feels like a verdict.

The air crackles with something greater than power—

something final.
