Step Into the Rift’s Depths
The Rift reveals all—do you shape it, or let it shape you?

The air around you quivers, thick with the weight of something unspoken, something ancient. The Rift pulses ahead, its shifting hues no longer violent, no longer chaotic, but measured—aware.

You are past the point of return.

There is no army at your back, no council debating your fate, no battle to be won or lost. The war, the choices, the conflicts of the world behind you—they all feel like echoes, distant and hollow. Here, there is only the Rift and what waits beyond it.

A pressure coils around you, pressing against your skin like unseen hands, not pushing, not pulling—testing.

A voice, not of words, but of thought, flickers at the edge of your mind.

You are not the first to come here.

The Rift shudders, and shadows stretch across the warped terrain. Figures emerge—blurred, shifting, as though caught between realities. Some wear armor, their forms flickering like unfinished thoughts. Others bear the robes of scholars, hands still grasping at knowledge lost to time. They are not whole. They are not broken.

They are remnants.

Lost souls, lost selves, reflections of what might have been, what could still be. They move like echoes of forgotten choices, splintered across time. And yet, in all of them, you recognize something familiar.

You.

You step forward. The Rift does not resist.

The moment your foot crosses its threshold, sensation fractures.

The world bends—not violently, not cruelly, but purposefully. Like the careful hands of a sculptor reshaping clay, the Rift molds itself around you, adjusting, rewriting, refining.

Reality no longer holds shape. You are falling—or rising. Time compresses, collapses, spreads outward. You see the echoes of past lives, past selves, converging into something singular.

The Rift is not a wound in reality.

It is not a mistake.

It is a design.

And you are part of it.

A shimmer of understanding blooms in your mind, unfolding like a thousand-layered puzzle revealing its final truth.

The Rift was always waiting.

Not for destruction.

Not for control.

For you.

For the first time, you have a choice no one before you has had.

Will you take this power—reshape reality in your image, become the force that others fear or worship?

Or will you step further, past the known, past the Rift itself, to uncover the true purpose behind it all?
