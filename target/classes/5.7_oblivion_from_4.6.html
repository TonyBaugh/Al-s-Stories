Oblivion – The Rift Consumes You

The Rift does not speak in words. It does not plead. It does not demand. It only waits.

And so, you demand the truth.

A whisper that is not a sound ripples through your thoughts, a vibration too deep, too old to be merely language. The Rift is not merely an abyss—it is a memory, a cycle without end, a force that has always been. You see it now, in the shifting, swirling depths. Every choice, every moment, every possibility you ever faced has been touched by it. The Rift is not an invader. It is not a mistake.

It is the architect of everything.

And yet, it is incomplete.

It stretches forward, not to consume, not to destroy—but to become.

You feel it pressing against you, flowing into the spaces between your thoughts, unraveling what you were and stitching something new in its place. The world outside flickers, distant and meaningless. What is reality but another form of confinement? What is identity but a name given to something ever-changing?

The Rift pulses, and the last remnants of your past peel away.

Your body is the first to dissolve—golden strands of light unravel from your fingertips, breaking into ribbons of shifting color. Your breath stills, yet you do not suffocate. Your heartbeat slows, yet you do not die. Your thoughts—your self—stretch outward, spilling into the Rift’s depths.

The sky, the ground, the very concept of direction loses meaning as you become weightless, timeless.

Memories flood in—fragments of lives that are not your own, pieces of history swallowed by the Rift, echoes of those who stood here before. They did not disappear. They did not end. They became part of it.

And now, so do you.

The last of your form shatters into color, dispersing into the infinite. Your name, your choices, your victories and failures—all of them are swallowed by the Rift, dissolving into its eternal hum.

~ ~ ~

The Rift does not devour.

The Rift remembers.

Summary – Rift Consumption

The Rift has claimed you, not as a victim, but as something far greater. You do not perish. You do not cease.

You become one with the Rift itself.

Absorbed into the endless cycle of existence and oblivion, your consciousness, your choices, and your very identity are scattered into the fabric of what has always been. The world may move on, but you are now part of something beyond time, beyond understanding.

No one will remember you.

But the Rift always will.

What This Means for the Player
  • This is the Oblivion Ending—a fate not of destruction, but of complete surrender to the unknown.
  • The Rift was never a force to be fought or controlled. It was always waiting for something to complete it.
  • By demanding the truth, you gave up your individual existence. You are not lost, but you are no longer you.
  • Unlike the Transcendence Ending, where you step beyond, this ending erases your identity into the Rift’s endless hum.
  • It is not death, not survival, but something entirely different.

Final Thought: You did not destroy the Rift. You did not escape it.

You became part of it.
