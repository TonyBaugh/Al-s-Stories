=== 1st Major Decision: Who Are You? ===

You feel the pressure of expectation from all around you. The faces watching you are filled with anticipation, terror, and something else - something unreadable. It is as though they already know what you will say, what you will choose, before the words even leave your lips.

But you don't know. Not yet.

A single decision could define everything. Or perhaps it will reveal nothing at all.

You cautiously nod, unsure of what else to do. The moment your head moves, the group erupts into chaos. Some cheer as if their savior has returned, others take a defensive stance as though you have confirmed their worst fears.

A man, clad in war-torn armor, steps forward, his expression an unsettling mix of relief and guilt.

"I knew it," he breathes. "You were always meant to lead us again."

But another voice cuts through the noise - a woman with sharp eyes and a dagger drawn.

"No. This is wrong. Last time, you nearly destroyed us all."

The world seems to tilt as two opposing realities press in on you. Were you a savior? A destroyer? Or neither?

The Rift hums behind you, pulsing as though it, too, is waiting for an answer.
